## Lesson compilation checklist

Before considering a lesson ready:

- the pain is recognizable
- the strategy is distinct
- the tactics point to real repo artifacts
- the proof shows behavior change, not abstract theory
- the lesson works for all three audiences
- the lesson reads like a public-facing explanation, not an internal planning note
