# Discovery Brief Template

Use this when no clear PRD exists yet or when the available source material is too fuzzy to drive implementation safely.

Default location:

- `docs/reference/prd-v0/10-discovery-brief.md`

## Purpose

Turn live discussion, pain points, or idea fragments into a durable first brief that future contributors can trust.

## Trigger or observed tension

- What pain point, bottleneck, or observed pattern started this discovery?

## Primary actor

- Who most needs the outcome?

## Desired outcome

- What do they really want to be true?
- What does success feel like from their perspective?
- What trust or quality bar must be met?

## Current state

- Where do they start?
- What tools, workarounds, or constraints already exist?

## Gap map

```txt
Current state -> obstacle or decision -> step -> step -> completion state
```

## Step decomposition

For each important step, capture:

- required action or decision
- required information
- required reassurance or proof
- automation opportunities
- risks, friction, or ambiguity

- direct competitor patterns worth borrowing
- adjacent or point-solution patterns worth borrowing
- cross-domain patterns worth testing
- negative patterns to avoid

- smallest meaningful implementation slice
- why this slice earns trust early
